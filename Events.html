Career Fair

Wednesday, March 6, 10:00am - 3:00pm

Maryville University

650 Maryville University Drive,

St. Louis, MO 63141

This will be our largest career fair ever with 80+ employers registered to attend who are looking forward to meeting talented students like you.

We hope your semester is off to an amazing start! The Office of Career Success & Professional Development is hosting the biggest career and networking event of the spring on Wednesday, March 6 from 10am-3pm which will provide an excellent opportunity to network with employers and learn about internship and employment opportunities. We will also have a photographer on-campus all day to take professional headshots for all students. ​Now is a great time to update your resume in preparation for the Career Fair! Please also review these career fair tips to maximize this experience and make a great impression. More...
